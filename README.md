This project provides an in-depth analysis of car sales performance across multiple regions, with the goal of identifying top-performing car categories, high-value customers, and countries driving the most revenue.

This project utilizes a combination of data transformation and visualization tools to deliver clear and actionable insights:

## üéØ Objectives  
- Identify the **best-selling car categories**  
- Highlight the **top 10 customers** by revenue  
- Analyze **sales performance by country and region**  
- Track **monthly average sales trends**

- Present key revenue KPIs for high-level decision-making

## üîç Process / Methodology  

### **1. Data Cleaning**
- Removed duplicates  
- Standardized customer, product, and location fields  
- Handled missing values  
- Ensured consistent data formatting  

### **2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**
Analyzed car sales across:
- Car categories  
- Countries and regions  
- Industry channels  
- Monthly sales trends  

### **3. Dashboard Development (Tableau)**  
Designed an interactive dashboard featuring:
- Revenue KPIs  
- Best-performing car categories

- Sales per country  
- Industry performance

---

## üìà Key Insights  
- **July and August** recorded the highest average sales, making them peak-performing months.  
- **Classic Cars** are the highest-selling category by a large margin, outperforming all other car types.  
- The **Euro Shopping Channel** is the most profitable industry segment to work with.  
- **Canada** drives the highest volume of sales across all countries.  
- **Japan, the Philippines, and Singapore** show significantly lower sales activity.  
- **November and December** (festive months) have unexpectedly low sales levels.
